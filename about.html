About Us

Mission

Expanding opportunities for emerging young professionals in economics to work with new and existing economic entities, governments and NGOs to solve developmental policy problems.

Aims and Objectives

  • To produce more confident, practice-ready young professionals equipped both theoretically and practically.
  • A strong network of future economists and policy makers sharing a common goal.
  • To instate well-groomed policy practitioners or forum associates in various economic institutions that align with our interests.
  • To make real contributions in solving current economic and public policy issues through stakeholder engagements.

What We Do

In this CoP, we strive to help early-career economists and policy-minded professionals connect learning with real-world action. We focus on understanding ideas, turning them into impact, and delivering hands-on solutions in our focus areas.

The Framework

  • Economic research in development: Asking smart questions and finding evidence-based answers to make informed decisions.
  • Policy formulation: Building informed policies based on research, public inputs, and real needs.
  • Public finance: Mobilizing and managing public resources for policy goals and sustainability.
  • Policy implementation: Turning policy from paper to practice to tackle real-world economic challenges.
  • Policy evaluation: Learning and assessing what works — and what doesn’t — to improve continuously.
