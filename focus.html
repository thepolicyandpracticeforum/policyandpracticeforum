Our Focus Areas

Driving research, policy innovation, and action to solve key developmental challenges.

Economic Research in Development

Asking smart questions and leveraging evidence to make informed decisions for sustainable growth.

Policy Formulation

Developing informed policies driven by research, public input, and deep stakeholder engagement.

Public Finance

Exploring resource mobilization, management, and sustainable financing to support policy goals.

Turning policy into practice to tackle developmental economic challenges using proven learning and engagement models.

Policy Evaluation

Assessing new and existing policies to understand what works, what doesn’t, and why — ensuring continuous improvement.

The Framework

  • Economic research in development: Asking smart questions and finding evidence-based answers in order to make informed decisions.
  • Policy formulation: Based on research efforts, needs, problems to be solved and public inputs (ideas and testimonies from people who are directly affected), we formulate informed policies.
  • Public finance: Exploring how public resources can be raised, managed and spent to carry out policies. This is about resource mobilization for the affairs of the forum and the economy.
  • Policy implementation: Moving policies from paper to practice to solve developmental economic challenges optimally — attained using learning and engagement models found in this CoP.
  • Policy evaluation: Practically evaluating new and existing policies to know what works, what does not, and why — also based on the learning and engagement models of the CoP.
